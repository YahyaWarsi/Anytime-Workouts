Jumping Jacks

1. Stand upright with your legs closed and arms to your side
2. Jump into the air and spread your legs shoulder-width apart. Stretch your arms above your head
3. Before landing from your jump, close your legs and put your arms to your side just as the initial position

Running On the Spot

1. Perform a light jog while remaining in the same spot

High Knees

1. Stand with your knees shoulder-width apart
2. Alternatively bring up your knees to waist level and carefully land your foot

Lunges

1. Stand straight with your feet shoulder width apart and hands on your hips
2. Take a big step infront with your right leg and go down till your right knee makes a 90 degree angle
3. Push on your leg and come back to the starting position
4. Perform the same movements for the next leg

Squats

1. Stand straight and keep your feet shoulder width apart
2. Extend your arms to help balance
Tip: Remember to put your weight on the back of your foot, not on the front of your foot. 3. Push your hips back
4. Squat down ensuring your knees are in-line with your ankles
5. Once your hips are are below your knees or parallel with your knees, come back up .

Side Lunges

1. Start with your hands on your hips and feet wider then shoulder-width
2. Take a large step to the side with your left foot and bend your left knee till it reaches a 90 degree angle
3. Push of your left foot and return to the starting position
4. Take a large step to the side with your right foot and bend your right knee till it reahes a 90 degree angle
5. Push of your right foot and return to the starting position

Reverse Lunge

1. Stand straight with your hands on your hips to help balance
2. Take a large step backward with your left foot
3. Bend your right knee till it's at a 90 degree angle and bend your left knee till it's at a 90 degree angle
4. Push on left foot to return to the neutral position .

Standing Elbow to Knee Crunch

1. Stand straight with your feet shoulder-width apart
2. Place both your hands on the back of your head
3. Make your left knee and right elbow touch by raising your left knee towards your right elbow and lowering your right elbow to touch your left knee
4. Do the same for your right knee and left elbow

Knee Push Ups

1. Start by placing your knees and hands on the ground
2. Extend your arms completely and place your hands shoulder-width apart
3. Bend your arms and lower your body until your chest touches or is near the ground
4. Push your body back up and straighten your arms

Wall Push Ups

1. Stand a couple feet away from a wall - the further you are from the wall the harder it is
2. While keeping your feet stationary, place both hands on the wall and keep them shoulder-width apart
3. Move your torso towards the wall and bend your arms
4. When your chest touches or is near the wall, push against the wall to straighten your arms

Leg Raise

1. Lay flat with your back on the ground
2. Press your hands on the ground to help stabilize yourself
3. Without bending your knees, raise your legs till they make a 90 degree angle with your body
4. Bring your legs down to the ground

Single Leg Donkey Kick

1. Place your knees and hands on the floor and extend your arms
2. Lift your right leg and slowly extend it upwards as much as you can
3. Bring your leg down slowly and place your knee on the ground
4. Do the same for your left leg

Butt Kicks

1. Stand straight and keep your feet shoulder-width apart
2. Bring your right heal to your buttocks and then place your foot back on the ground
3. Do the same with your left foot
4. Gradually increase your speed

Side to Side Punches

1. Stand straight with your feet shoulder-width apart.
2. Punch forward while twisting your torso

Overhead Arm Claps

1. Either sitting on standing, start with your arms by your side
2. Without bending your elbow clap your hands above you head

Arm Circless

1. Stand straight with your feet shoulder width apart
2. Extend your arms on the sides and move them in a circular motion
3. Start with rotating your arms in a small circular motion and gradually increase

Side-Lying Hip Abduction

1. Lie on one side of your body
2. Raise your leg upwards slowly, then bring it down slowly
3. After 20 seconds, switch sides and perform the action for the other side

Standard Crunch

1. Lay on your back with your feet on the ground
2. Put your hands on your head or accross your chest, contract your abs and inhale
3. Exhale and bring your body up - Remeber to use your abdomen to bring yourself up so ensure you relax your neck and head
4. Come back down to the starting position

1. Start in a plank position, with your hands and feet on the ground, hands shoulder-width apart and body straight
2. Tap your right hand with your left knee, then set your right hand back to its starting spot

Tuck Jump

4. Straighten your legs and land on your feet
