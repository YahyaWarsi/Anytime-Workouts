What is Anytime Workouts?

Anytime Workouts is a web application designed for everyone of all ages and fitness levels. The goal of this app is to eliminate the hassle of finding or creating a workout for yourself. With Anytime Workouts, you can start working out within seconds for as long as you want!

How to use Anytime Workouts

  • Click the green "START WORKOUT!" button below.
  • A prompt will appear asking which difficulty level you prefer. You may choose a, beginner, b, intermediate and c, advanced. Once entered, click "ok" to continue.
  • Another prompt will appear asking the desired workout duration. Here you may enter how long you want to workout for. The workout duration can only be from 1 minute to 59 minutes. Once entered, click "ok" to continue.
  • To begin your workout, click the blue "Begin Workout" button.
  • Before each exercise begins, there is a 10 second break.
  • When the 10 second timer is over, begin the exercise shown.
  • To pause and resume, click the "Pause" and "Resume" button.
  • Keep going until the timer reaches zero.

Features

  • Fast and easy to use
  • 3 difficulty levels for everyone at all fitness levels
  • Workout for 1 minute or 59 minutes, you can choose the duration of your workout based on how much time you have
  • 50+ exercises in total and 15+ exercises at each difficulty level
  • Pause and resume your workout
  • Background changes color to red during the workout to boost your energy levels and green to cool you down when not workout out
